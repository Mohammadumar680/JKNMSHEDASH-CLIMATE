What is NMSHE?

🏔️

Part of India's National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC)

One of 8 National Missions under NAPCC, dedicated to protecting the fragile Himalayan ecosystem from accelerating climate change impacts.

NMSHE (National Mission on Sustaining the Himalayan Ecosystem) is a Government of India mission under the National Action Plan on Climate Change (NAPCC), aimed at protecting the Himalayan region by understanding and addressing the impacts of climate change while promoting sustainable development.


The mission monitors climate variability, glaciers and snow cover, water resources, biodiversity, and disaster risks — including floods, landslides, and glacial lake outburst floods (GLOFs) — using advanced tools like Remote Sensing, GIS, and data analytics.


In Jammu & Kashmir, NMSHE contributes through glacier retreat and snow decline studies, district-wise climate trend analysis, Web-GIS dashboards, wetland monitoring, and science-based policy support for the state government and DST.

🛰️ Remote Sensing & GIS

Satellite-based monitoring of glaciers, snow cover, and land use changes across the Himalayan region.

📊 Climate Data Analytics

District-wise temperature and rainfall trend analysis using long-term meteorological datasets.

🌊 GLOF Risk Assessment

Identification and monitoring of glacial lakes at high risk of outburst floods in J&K highlands.

🗺️ Web-GIS Dashboards

Interactive digital platforms including Power BI SAPCC dashboard for policy support.

🌿 Wetland Monitoring

Ecosystem health assessment of J&K wetlands — Wular Lake, Hokersar and Ramsar sites.

👥 Capacity Building

Training workshops and awareness programs building climate-resilient governance in J&K.

Mission Pillars

Core Objectives

Five foundational pillars guide NMSHE's approach to protecting the Himalayan ecosystem and building climate resilience across Jammu & Kashmir.

🏔️
Monitoring Himalayan Ecosystems
+
Continuous observation of glaciers, snow cover, temperature, rainfall, water resources, forests, and natural hazards using remote sensing satellites, GIS, climate models, and field observations. Provides scientific evidence for climate adaptation, disaster risk reduction, and conservation planning across all 20 J&K districts.
🌱
Sustainable Development
+
Balancing economic growth, environmental protection, and social well-being. In fragile Himalayan regions this involves climate-resilient agriculture, sustainable tourism, ecosystem conservation, disaster-risk reduction, and science-based planning — ensuring natural resources are available for future generations.
⚠️
Disaster Risk Reduction
+
Systematic identification, assessment, and reduction of disaster risks including floods, landslides, GLOFs, and avalanches. Activities include hazard mapping, early warning systems, vulnerability assessment, and land-use planning using Remote Sensing and GIS — enhancing community resilience across steep Himalayan terrain.
🔬
Research & Technology
+
Application of remote sensing satellites, GIS, climate models, data analytics, and field-based observations to monitor ecosystems and predict future changes. Research findings drive evidence-based decision-making, policy formulation, and long-term environmental conservation strategies for the DST and state government.
🤝
Community Awareness & Training
+
Educating and empowering local communities through workshops, awareness campaigns, school programs, and hands-on training in disaster preparedness, water conservation, sustainable livelihoods, and ecosystem protection. Local people serve as first responders during disasters and primary stewards of natural resources.

Sun rays enter... heat gets trapped! सूरज की किरणें आती हैं... गर्मी फँस जाती है! سورج کی کرنیں آتی ہیں... گرمی پھنس جاتی ہے!
Like a blanket around Earth — CO₂ and other gases trap heat and warm our planet. धरती के चारों ओर एक कंबल की तरह — CO₂ और अन्य गैसें गर्मी को रोकती हैं और हमारी धरती को गर्म करती हैं। زمین کے چاروں طرف کمبل کی طرح — CO₂ اور دیگر گیسیں گرمی کو روکتی ہیں اور ہماری زمین کو گرم کرتی ہیں۔

This is an emergency. जलवायु हमेशा से प्राकृतिक रूप से बदलती रही है, लेकिन आज जो हो रहा है वह 95% मनुष्यों की वजह से है। हम जीवाश्म ईंधन जला रहे हैं और जंगल काट रहे हैं — जितना प्रकृति सह सकती है उससे 100 गुना तेज़। यह एक आपातकाल है। موسم ہمیشہ قدرتی طور پر تبدیل ہوتا رہا ہے، لیکن آج جو ہو رہا ہے وہ 95% انسانوں کی وجہ سے ہے۔ ہم جیواشم ایندھن جلا رہے ہیں اور جنگل کاٹ رہے ہیں — اس رفتار سے جو قدرت برداشت نہیں کر سکتی۔ یہ ایک ایمرجنسی ہے۔

🦠 Diseases 🦠 बीमारियाँ 🦠 بیماریاں

Plant Trees पेड़ लगाएं درخت لگائیں
Plant 1 tree/month. Trees absorb CO₂ and give cool shade महीने में 1 पेड़ लगाएं। पेड़ CO₂ सोखते हैं और ठंडी छाया देते हैं مہینے میں 1 درخت لگائیں۔ درخت CO₂ جذب کرتے اور ٹھنڈی چھاؤں دیتے ہیں

Solar Energy सौर ऊर्जा شمسی توانائی
Use solar panels and solar cookers — free energy from sun! सोलर पैनल और सोलर कुकर का उपयोग करें — सूरज से मुफ्त ऊर्जा! سولر پینل اور سولر کوکر استعمال کریں — سورج سے مفت توانائی!

Save Water पानी बचाएं پانی بچائیں
Fix leaks, collect rainwater, drip irrigation for crops रिसाव ठीक करें, बारिश का पानी जमा करें, फसलों के लिए ड्रिप सिंचाई رساؤ ٹھیک کریں، بارش کا پانی جمع کریں، فصلوں کے لیے ڈرپ آبپاشی

Reduce Waste कचरा कम करें کچرا کم کریں
Don't burn garbage — compost, recycle, reuse everything possible कचरा मत जलाएं — खाद बनाएं, पुनर्चक्रण करें, हर चीज़ दोबारा उपयोग करें کچرا مت جلائیں — کھاد بنائیں، ری سائیکل کریں، ہر چیز دوبارہ استعمال کریں
🚲
Walk & Cycle चलें व साइकिल پیدل چلیں، سائیکل
Walk or cycle for short distances — no pollution, good health! कम दूरी के लिए पैदल चलें या साइकिल — कोई प्रदूषण नहीं, अच्छा स्वास्थ्य! کم فاصلے کے لیے پیدل چلیں یا سائیکل — کوئی آلودگی نہیں، اچھی صحت!
🌾
Organic Farming जैविक खेती نامیاتی کھیتی
Use natural fertilizers — no chemicals, better soil, less gas प्राकृतिक खाद का उपयोग करें — कोई रसायन नहीं, बेहतर मिट्टी, कम गैस قدرتی کھاد استعمال کریں — کوئی کیمیکل نہیں، بہتر مٹی، کم گیس
💡
Save Electricity बिजली बचाएं بجلی بچائیں
Switch off lights/fans when not needed. Use LED bulbs ज़रूरत न हो तो लाइट/पंखा बंद करें। LED बल्ब का इस्तेमाल करें ضرورت نہ ہو تو لائٹ/پنکھا بند کریں۔ LED بلب استعمال کریں
